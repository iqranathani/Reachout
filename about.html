WHO WE ARE

We aim to be Pakistan's first professional suicide prevention and mental health helpline. We are an NGO run by amazing volunteers who are here for you 24/7. Our work is based on the foundation and belief that suicide is preventable and everyone has a role to play in preventing suicide. Through raising public awareness, educating communities, and equipping every person with the right tools, we know we can SAVE lives.

OUR MISSION

To prevent suicide through public awareness and education, reduce stigma and serve as a resource to those touched by suicide.
